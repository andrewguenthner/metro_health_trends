Summary of Trends

Trends in health indicators from 2011-2017 for 259 metropolitan and micropolitan areas were summarized according to the following classification scheme.

  • Positive trend (in terms of health), improvement accelerating
  • Positive trend (in terms of health), improvement steady
  • Positive trend (in terms of health), improvement slowing / reversing
  • No discernible trend
  • Negative trend (in terms of health), decline slowing / reversing
  • Negative trend (in terms of health), decline steady
  • Negative trend (in terms of health), decline accelerating
